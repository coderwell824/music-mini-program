flex: (flex-grow)值 (flex-shrink)值 (flex-basis)值

flex-grow     --可拉伸  默认值为0
flex-shrink   --可压缩  默认值为1
flex-basis    --当前元素的宽度  默认值为auto

flex:1
--->flex-grow :1    flex-shrink :1    flex-basis :0%
会导致父元素的宽度自动成为100%

flex:auto
--->flex-grow :1    flex-shrink :1    flex-basis :auto
